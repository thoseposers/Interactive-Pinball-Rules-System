Frank Comes Alive

Assemble Frankenstein by hitting his targets six times. Frankenstein will then raise up and allow you to shoot his ramp to begin his multiball.

During multiball, lit shots award jackpots. Hitting Frank awards a super, double super, or triple super based on if a number of normal jackpots were shot first.

Get Frank's keyboard by hitting six super, double, or triple jackpots. (Required for Monsters of Rock)
