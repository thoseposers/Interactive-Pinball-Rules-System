Bonus

Bonus starts at 0 and increases by 1000 points each time a Target Bank's drop target or lit Spinner stand up is hit.

When one drop target remains in a bank, score lit bonus will be lit. Clearing the final drop target will score the current bonus value.

Bonus increases by 3000 by each outlane and right side run-way lane.

When the ball drains, the bonus score (times the multiplier) is collected and then reset to 0.
