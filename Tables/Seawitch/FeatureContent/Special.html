Special

Specials light when 6X is reached.

Outlanes score 5000 and advances bonus 3000. Special is awarded if lit.

Shooting the run-way lane on the right that leads to the shooter lane scores 5000 and advances bonus 3000. Special is awarded if lit.

Special scores one replay by default. Settings can be changed to award nothing, extra ball, 100,000, or a replay.

Special location changes with every Sling hit.
