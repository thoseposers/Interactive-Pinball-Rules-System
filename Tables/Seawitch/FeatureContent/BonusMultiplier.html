Bonus Multiplier

Bonus mutliplier is advanced when a Target Bank's drop targets are cleared while the associated increase X insert is lit.

The increase X insert cycles from center Target Bank, to left, to top, then back to center.

The maximum bonus multiplier is 7X.

At the end of a ball, bonus multiplier up to 5X is carried over to the next ball. When 6X or 7X is reached, bonus multiplier is reset to 3X and can be advanced again.
