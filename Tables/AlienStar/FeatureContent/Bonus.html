Bonus

Bonus starts at 1000 and maxes out at 29,000.

Inlanes, S-T-A-R Lanes and blinking A-L-I-E-N targets advance Bonus once.

When the ball drains, the Bonus score (times the multiplier) is collected and then reset to 1000.

Bonus can also be collected during a ball by rolling through the Collect Bonus top lane while the S letter and Collect Bonus S-T-A-R Lane award are lit.
