Bonus Multiplier

Advance your Bonus Multiplier up to 5X by shooting the Advance Bonus Multiplier target while the inserts are flashing.

The Advance Bonus Multiplier inserts light any time the ball passes through the left inlane

Hitting the Advance Bonus Multiplier target while the inserts are not flashing does not advance the multiplier and only scores 3000.

Additionally, the Bonus Multiplier can be advanced by rolling through the Advance Multiplier top lane while the T letter and Advance Multiplier S-T-A-R Lane award are lit.
