A-Z-T-E-C Letters

Collect A-Z-T-E-C Letters by shooting the marked targets and top center lane. Each worth 1000 points.

Shoot the U-turn on the right to score 10,000 for each A-Z-T-E-C letter you have collected. 5000 points are earned if no letters have been collected.

The center target awards 1000 points for each letter collected, but only 500 points if no letters have been collected
